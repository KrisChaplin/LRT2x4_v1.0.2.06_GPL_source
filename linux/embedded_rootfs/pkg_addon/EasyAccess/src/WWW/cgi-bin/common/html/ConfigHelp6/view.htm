View Certificates

You may view the certificates you have uploaded to the EasyAccess appliance. You may also change the certificate password and delete certificates from the View Certificates window.

 

  1. To view and change certificate settings, click General in the left navigation menu, then click Certificates.
     

  2. The Current Certificates table at the bottom of the window lists the currently loaded SSL certificates. To view certificate and issuer information, click the View link for the certificate you wish to view. A new View Certificate window will be displayed.
     

  3. From the View Certificate window, you may view the issuer and certificate subject information.
     

  4. Update the certificate password by entering the correct password in the Certificate Password field. Click Submit to submit the changes.

 

You may also delete an expired or incorrect certificate. Delete the certificate by clicking the Delete button.
